What is Meetings Simplified?

Meetings Simplified is a web application that helps you understand your audience better with a simple survey. The simple process begins when you create a short survey form to send out to your colleagues. You can ask them to rate how comfortable they are with a particular topic, and we'll take that information to give you some advice and feedback on what to focus on during your presentation or meeting.
